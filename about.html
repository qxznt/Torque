Our Story
Born on the production floor. Built for it.

Torque Robotics & Solutions was founded by engineers who spent years working directly on automotive production lines — not in boardrooms or design offices, but in the thick of live commissioning, deadline-driven ramp-ups, and real-world robot troubleshooting. That origin defines everything we do.

We started with a simple belief: the industry needed a partner that could move at the speed of the factory floor. A team that understood not just the technology, but the pressure, the timelines, and the uncompromising quality expectations that automotive manufacturing demands.

Simulation-First Philosophy

Every project begins in the digital world before it touches the factory floor. Our simulation engineers build complete digital twins — validating robot paths, cycle times, and interference zones before a single physical component is installed. This eliminates expensive surprises during commissioning and keeps your project on schedule.

OEM-Grade Expertise

We have worked shoulder-to-shoulder with engineers from Maruti Suzuki, Tata Motors, Honda, Hyundai, Renault-Nissan, Ford, and General Motors. Our team understands their weld quality standards, safety protocols, and documentation requirements — not from reading manuals, but from years of onsite delivery across BIW and non-BIW processes.

Global Reach, Local Speed

From Gurgaon to Dubai, South Korea to the UK — Torque Robotics has mobilised teams across 10+ countries, bringing the same technical rigour and responsiveness regardless of geography. Our ability to deploy quickly and operate across time zones has made us a trusted partner for global automotive expansions.

Frequently Asked Questions
Everything you need to know about Torque Robotics & Solutions
What industries does Torque Robotics serve?
Torque Robotics primarily serves the automotive manufacturing sector, working with leading OEMs and Tier-1 suppliers including Maruti Suzuki, Tata Motors, Hyundai, Honda, Renault-Nissan, JBM Auto, Caparo, and DICV. Our expertise spans BIW (Body-in-White) and Non-BIW processes across India and globally.
Which robot brands does Torque Robotics support?
Our engineers are trained and certified across all major industrial robot platforms — Fanuc, ABB, KUKA, Yaskawa, Nachi, and Kawasaki. This multi-brand expertise means we can work seamlessly on any production line regardless of the installed robot fleet.
What simulation software does your team use?
Our simulation practice uses industry-leading tools including Siemens Process Simulate, Tecnomatix Plant Simulation, DELMIA V5, Roboguide (Fanuc), RobotStudio (ABB), and KUKA Sim. This allows us to deliver accurate OLP (Offline Programs) and digital validation before any physical work begins on the production floor.
How many projects has Torque Robotics completed?
We have successfully delivered over 500 projects across India's most demanding automotive production environments. Our portfolio spans cycle time optimisation, new line commissioning, robot integration into existing lines, and full turnkey system integration for global OEMs and their Tier-1 suppliers.
Does Torque Robotics offer on-site support across India?
Yes. We have a nationwide deployment capability with offices in Gurgaon (Head Office), Manesar, Gujarat, and Pune. Our field engineers are routinely deployed to client facilities across all major automotive manufacturing hubs in India, and we also support international project deployments.
How do I engage Torque Robotics for a project?
You can reach us directly at sales@torquerobotics.in or use the Contact Us form on our website. Our team will review your requirements and schedule a technical discussion. For urgent production issues, we offer rapid-response deployment within 24–48 hours depending on location.
